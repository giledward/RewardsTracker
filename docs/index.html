How to read the page

This is not just a price board. It is a relationship board. The goal is to answer: is BONK moving because SOL is moving, or because BONK itself is gaining strength?

High correlation: SOL is probably driving BONK direction.
BONK outperforming: BONK has relative strength versus SOL.
Low correlation: BONK may be moving from its own catalysts, rewards, holders, or meme cycle.
